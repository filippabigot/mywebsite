Finding exiting things to do in a new city can be hard sometimes. That's why we've made this website, to make it easy for you to find fun things to do while staying in Jönköping. There are lots of things to experience while being here. Visit a museum with the family? Travel to the beautiful town Gränna? Or why not jump in the water of lake Vättern? Whatever gets you going, we provide the answers right here.

What do to

Visit museums such as:

  • Jönköping Läns Museum
  • The Matchmuseum
  • Husqvarna Museum

Join Dreamhack Winter/Summer

the worlds largest digital festival, which is arrenged in Jönköping twice a year. Be part of the incredible event that attracts millions of people from all over the world, online and in real life.

Take a walk through Rosenlunds rosarium:

Rosenlund rosarium is beautifully located in an old cultural environment within walkning distance to the beach of lake Vättern. As well as large numbers of beautiful roses, there are exotic trees and areas planted with perennials.

Hike through Dumme Mosse

Go on a delightful like on planked trails in southern Sweden's last wilderness. The air here is filles with the scent of marsh Labrador tea, and animal and plant life abounds. A good hike for the active person as well as the nature lover.
